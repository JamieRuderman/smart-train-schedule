# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-24

## Summary
- No account is required to use the app.
- We do not collect or sell personal information.
- Schedule data is fetched from our public server.

## Information We Collect
The SMART trip app does not collect personal information. We do not request your
name, email, location, or other identifiers.

### Anonymous Analytics (Web Only)
The web version of the app may use Vercel Analytics to collect aggregated,
anonymous usage metrics. This does not run inside the native Android or iOS apps.

## How We Use Information
Any anonymous analytics data is used only to understand general usage trends and
improve the app.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

## Third-Party Services
The app retrieves schedule data from our hosted public endpoint. No personal
data is sent with these requests.

## Children's Privacy
This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect
personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page
with a revised effective date.
